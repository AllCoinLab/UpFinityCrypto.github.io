How to Stake

  • Connect your wallet
  • Write down 'Amount to Stake'
  • Click 'Stake'
  • Confirm transaction in wallet dapp
  • UPF will be staked

How to Unstake

  • Connect your wallet
  • Click 'Unstake'
  • Confirm transaction in wallet dapp
  • UPF will be unstaked with rewards

How to change staked amount

  • You should unstake and stake again

  • 1 day: 1M UPF
  • 1 week: 10M UPF
  • 1 month: 100M UPF
  • 1 year: 500M UPF
